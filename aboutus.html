About us

Phil-Orient Pharma and Medical Supplies Trading is a start-up pharmaceutical and medical supplies company based in Cebu, Philippines, with a starting scope of the Visayas and Mindanao Region. The company is being led by a team of veteran medical representatives coming from both the pharmaceutical industry and the medical supplies industry. The company is engaged in importing, wholesaling, and distribution of various pharmaceutical and medical supplies products ranging from ethical and OTC medicines, to medical equipment, to hospital needs and many more.

MISSION

At Phil-Orient Pharma, we are dedicated to enhancing lives through the provision of top-quality pharmaceutical and medical device products. Committed to excellence, integrity, and innovation, we strive to be a trusted partner in healthcare, contributing to a healthier and brighter future for all.

VISION

Phil-Orient Pharma envisions leading global healthcare transformation through innovation and excellence. Our goal is to pioneer advanced solutions, fostering healthier communities and setting industry standards, ensuring universal access to cutting-edge healthcare.
