About Us

Phil-Orient Pharma and Medical Supplies Trading is a start-up pharmaceutical and medical supplies company based in Cebu, Philippines, with a starting scope of the Visayas and Mindanao Region. The company is being led by a team of veteran medical representatives coming from both the pharmaceutical industry and the medical supplies industry.
